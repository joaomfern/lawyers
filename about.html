Pedro Cunha

Pedro Mendes da Cunha is a portuguese lawyer having experience working as in house lawyer for an international portuguese IT company and a renewable energy company, having worked with multiple jurisdictions.

Currently he advises foreign and national companies set up business in Portugal, complying with local regulations and advises clients on immigration and investment matters.

He holds e in International Relations from the University of Porto, a LLM in Globalisation and Law – International Economic Law from the University of Maastricht and a Law Bachelor’s Degree from the Catholic University of Porto.

He speaks Portuguese, English and Spanish fluently and has elementary knowledge of French.

His main fields of interest are in Public International and European Law, Immigration, Real Estate and Corporate Law.

Rafaela Verdi
